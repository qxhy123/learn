# 第21章：段落结构与衔接

> "A paragraph should be like a miniature essay — with a beginning, a middle, and an end. The topic sentence is the thesis; the supporting sentences are the body; the concluding sentence wraps it up."
> — William Strunk Jr. & E.B. White, *The Elements of Style*

---

## 引言

写作的基本单元不是句子，而是**段落（paragraph）**。一篇文章的质量，在很大程度上取决于每个段落内部的结构是否严密、思维是否连贯、证据是否充分。许多中国学生在学习英语写作时，往往能写出语法正确的句子，却无法组织出清晰有力的段落——句子是砖块，但砖块之间没有灰浆，墙壁就会倒塌。

本章将系统讲解英语段落的结构、展开方法、衔接手段和常见问题，帮助你从"写句子"的阶段跨越到"写段落"的阶段，最终实现"写文章"的目标。

---

## 21.1 段落的定义与功能

### 什么是段落？

在英语写作中，**一个段落只能表达一个核心思想（one paragraph = one central idea）**。这是英美写作教学中最基础也最重要的原则。

一个标准段落由以下三个部分构成：

| 组成部分 | 英文术语 | 功能 |
|---|---|---|
| 主题句 | Topic Sentence | 提出本段的核心主张或论点 |
| 支撑句 | Supporting Sentences | 通过例证、解释、数据等展开主题 |
| 结论句 | Concluding Sentence | 总结本段内容，呼应或升华主题 |

### 段落的四大功能

1. **表达功能**：清晰传递一个完整的思想单元
2. **组织功能**：将文章切割成可管理的逻辑块
3. **引导功能**：帮助读者追踪作者的思维路径
4. **视觉功能**：通过缩进和空行为读者提供阅读节奏

### 段落长度的参考标准

段落没有硬性的字数要求，但有实践参考：

- **学术写作**：通常150-300词，约8-15句
- **商务写作**：通常50-150词，约3-8句
- **新闻写作**：通常1-3句，极简风格
- **创意写作**：长短不一，服务于表达节奏

一个常见的错误是段落过短（只有1-2句，思想未展开）或过长（超过500词，包含多个核心思想，应该分段）。

## 21.2 主题句（Topic Sentence）的写法

### 主题句的位置

主题句最常见的位置是**段落开头**，这是英美写作的主流规范，也是学术写作和考试写作中最推荐的做法。但主题句也可以出现在其他位置：

**① 段首主题句（最常见）**

直接亮出观点，后续句子展开支撑：

```
Social media has fundamentally changed the way young people communicate.
Instead of face-to-face conversations, teenagers now rely on text messages
and emoji to express emotions. A 2023 study by Harvard found that 68% of
Generation Z prefer texting over calling, even with close friends.
```

**② 段中主题句（悬念式）**

前几句铺垫，主题句出现在中间：

```
Teenagers spend hours scrolling through Instagram. They text friends
rather than calling. They attend virtual hangouts instead of meeting
in person. This shift toward digital-first communication represents
a fundamental change in how young people relate to one another.
The implications for social development are only beginning to be understood.
```

**③ 段尾主题句（归纳式）**

前面列举事实或现象，最后提炼结论：

```
In a recent survey, 72% of college students admitted to checking
their phones during lectures. A quarter confessed to texting during
family dinners. Many report feeling anxious when separated from their
devices for more than an hour. Clearly, smartphone dependency has become
a defining characteristic of contemporary youth culture.
```

### 好的主题句的三个特征

**特征一：明确（Specific）**

主题句必须传达一个具体可辨的立场，而不是模糊的泛论。

**特征二：可控（Controllable）**

主题句所涵盖的范围必须在一个段落内能够充分展开，既不能太宽泛，也不能太狭窄。

**特征三：有方向（Directional）**

好的主题句暗示了后续展开的方向，让读者能预期接下来的内容。

### 10个好/坏主题句对比

| # | 差的主题句 | 问题分析 | 好的主题句 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Climate change is a problem. | 过于宽泛，无法在一段内展开 | Rising sea levels threaten the existence of three Pacific island nations. |
| 2 | This paragraph is about education. | 直接宣告话题，不是观点 | Inadequate funding forces many rural schools to cut arts programs entirely. |
| 3 | There are many reasons why people exercise. | 方向模糊，无从展开 | Regular aerobic exercise reduces the risk of depression by up to 30%. |
| 4 | Shakespeare wrote many plays. | 陈述众所周知的事实，没有论点 | Shakespeare's use of female disguise in *Twelfth Night* exposes the performative nature of gender. |
| 5 | The economy is complicated. | 太模糊，没有具体立场 | The 2008 financial crisis disproportionately destroyed the savings of middle-class families. |
| 6 | I will discuss the benefits of meditation. | 宣告写作意图而非直接表达观点 | Daily meditation of just ten minutes significantly improves emotional regulation in adolescents. |
| 7 | Technology is good and bad. | 两面都说，缺乏立场 | Over-reliance on GPS navigation is eroding the human capacity for spatial reasoning. |
| 8 | Many countries face immigration issues. | 泛泛而谈，缺乏切入点 | Canada's points-based immigration system has successfully attracted high-skilled workers without increasing unemployment. |
| 9 | In this essay, I want to talk about fast food. | 元评论式（meta-commentary），学术写作大忌 | The fast food industry's marketing to children under 12 constitutes a form of predatory advertising. |
| 10 | Dogs make good pets for many reasons. | 平淡无力，无方向感 | Owning a dog has been clinically shown to lower blood pressure and reduce feelings of loneliness in elderly adults. |

## 21.3 支撑句（Supporting Sentences）的展开方法

主题句提出"是什么"，支撑句回答"为什么"和"怎么证明"。优秀的段落通常综合使用多种展开方法。

### 六大展开方法

#### ① 举例（Example）

最常见的展开方式。例子可以是具体事件、人物、数字或情境。

```
The rise of remote work has transformed urban living patterns.
For example, cities like Austin, Texas, and Boise, Idaho, have
experienced dramatic population surges as workers flee expensive
coastal metros. One Austin resident, Maria Chen, relocated from
San Francisco in 2021, cutting her rent from $3,800 to $1,400 per month
while keeping her Silicon Valley salary intact.
```

引导例证的常用表达：
- `For example, ...` / `For instance, ...`
- `To illustrate, ...`
- `A case in point is ...`
- `Consider the case of ...`
- `Take ... as an example.`

#### ② 解释（Explanation）

对主题句中的概念、术语或主张进行阐释说明：

```
Confirmation bias significantly distorts our perception of reality.
This cognitive tendency — the inclination to seek out information
that confirms our pre-existing beliefs while ignoring contradictory
evidence — operates largely below conscious awareness. When a person
already believes, for instance, that vaccines are dangerous, they
will unconsciously notice and remember stories of adverse reactions
while dismissing the overwhelming statistical evidence of safety.
```

#### ③ 数据（Statistics）

用数字、研究发现、调查结果增强说服力：

```
Sleep deprivation has reached epidemic proportions among teenagers.
According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, over 70%
of high school students in the United States fail to get the recommended
eight to ten hours of sleep per night. A 2022 meta-analysis of 28 studies
found that sleep-deprived adolescents scored an average of 15 percentile
points lower on standardized cognitive tests than their well-rested peers.
```

使用数据的注意事项：
- 注明数据来源（哪个机构、哪一年）
- 将抽象数字具体化（"15 percentile points lower"比"significantly lower"更有力）
- 不要堆砌数字，一段中1-2个核心数据足够

#### ④ 对比（Comparison）

通过与其他事物的比较揭示特征：

```
The human brain's capacity for language acquisition diminishes sharply
after early childhood. Unlike adults, who must consciously memorize
grammar rules and vocabulary, children under seven absorb language
effortlessly through immersive exposure. A child growing up bilingual
will achieve native-level fluency in both languages with no formal
instruction, a feat virtually impossible for an adult learner regardless
of motivation or intelligence.
```

对比句型：
- `Unlike A, B ...`
- `While A ..., B ...`
- `In contrast to A, B ...`
- `Whereas A ..., B ...`

#### ⑤ 因果（Cause-Effect）

解释现象背后的原因，或预测可能导致的后果：

```
The collapse of biodiversity in agricultural regions stems directly
from monoculture farming practices. When farmers plant a single crop
species across thousands of acres, they eliminate the varied habitats
that pollinators, birds, and small mammals depend upon. The result is
a cascading ecological collapse: without insects, birds disappear;
without birds, pest populations explode; and without pest control,
farmers are forced to apply ever-greater quantities of pesticides,
further poisoning the environment.
```

因果句型：
- `... because ...` / `... since ...` / `... as ...`
- `... leads to ...` / `... results in ...` / `... causes ...`
- `As a result, ...` / `Consequently, ...` / `Therefore, ...`
- `Due to ...` / `Owing to ...` / `As a consequence of ...`

#### ⑥ 引用（Quotation）

援引权威观点、研究者言论或文学作品：

```
The psychological damage of poverty extends far beyond material
deprivation. As Nobel laureate economist Amartya Sen argues,
"Poverty is not just a lack of money; it is not having the capability
to realize one's full potential as a human being." This insight helps
explain why simply distributing cash payments to poor communities,
while helpful, cannot by itself break cycles of generational poverty —
the erosion of aspiration and self-efficacy requires far more targeted
social interventions.
```

引用注意事项：
- 引用后必须解释其与论点的关联（不能引用后不分析）
- 直接引用不超过段落的20%
- 引用要有来源归属（attribution）

## 21.4 结论句（Concluding Sentence）的收束技巧

结论句是段落的最后一句，其功能是总结、呼应或升华——而不是引入新信息。

### 五种结论句写法

**① 重述主题（Restatement）**——用不同词汇复述主题句的核心意思

```
Topic sentence: Social media addiction is rewiring adolescent brains.
Concluding sentence: The neurological impact of compulsive social media use
on developing minds, in short, cannot be overstated.
```

**② 归纳总结（Summary）**——简洁总结支撑句的核心要点

```
Taken together, these biological, psychological, and social factors
paint a deeply troubling picture of how digital platforms exploit
adolescent vulnerability.
```

**③ 引出含义（Implication）**——指出该段论点的更大意义

```
If these trends continue unchecked, we may be raising the first
generation of humans whose primary emotional bonds are with algorithms
rather than other people.
```

**④ 衔接下段（Transition）**——为下一段做铺垫

```
Understanding why adolescents are vulnerable to social media addiction
is only the first step; the more pressing question is what parents,
educators, and policymakers can do about it.
```

**⑤ 呼吁行动（Call to Action）**——适用于说服性写作

```
These findings demand immediate regulatory action before another
generation grows up as unwitting subjects in a global psychological experiment.
```

### 结论句的禁忌

- 不要用 `In conclusion, ...` 或 `In summary, ...`（这是整篇文章结论段的用语）
- 不要引入新的论点或信息
- 不要重复主题句的原文（要用同义替换）

## 21.5 段落内的衔接（Cohesion）

**衔接（Cohesion）** 是指段落内部的句子之间通过语言手段形成有机连接。没有衔接的段落，即使每句话都正确，读起来也像一串孤立的句子，而不是一个整体。

### 衔接词分类表

#### Addition（递进/补充）

| 衔接词 | 语域 | 用法说明 |
|---|---|---|
| Furthermore | 正式 | 补充更重要的观点，语气强于 moreover |
| Moreover | 正式 | 添加同等重要的额外信息 |
| In addition | 中性偏正式 | 最通用的"此外"，使用频率极高 |
| Additionally | 中性 | 与 in addition 同义，更简洁 |
| Besides | 非正式 | 口语化，学术写作中慎用 |
| What is more | 正式 | 强调追加信息，常用于书面语 |
| Also | 中性 | 最简单的"也"，通常不放句首 |
| Not only ... but also | 正式 | 强调两点，第二点更重要 |

#### Contrast（对比/转折）

| 衔接词 | 语域 | 用法说明 |
|---|---|---|
| However | 正式 | 最标准的转折词，可放句首或句中 |
| Nevertheless | 正式 | 语气更强的"尽管如此"，承认前文但转折 |
| Nonetheless | 正式 | 与 nevertheless 同义，稍显书面 |
| In contrast | 正式 | 专门用于明确对比两个不同事物 |
| On the other hand | 中性 | 呈现另一面观点，不一定有对错之分 |
| Yet | 中性 | 简洁有力的转折，常用于书面 |
| Although / Though | 中性 | 引导让步状语从句，不能单独成句 |
| Despite / In spite of | 正式 | 后接名词短语，not 从句 |
| Whereas | 正式 | 对比两个并列事实，客观中立 |
| While | 中性 | 可表对比，也表同时，根据语境判断 |

#### Cause-Effect（因果）

| 衔接词 | 语域 | 用法说明 |
|---|---|---|
| Therefore | 正式 | 最标准的"因此"，逻辑严谨 |
| Thus | 正式 | 学术写作常用，更简洁 |
| Hence | 正式偏古典 | 在现代学术写作中仍常见 |
| Consequently | 正式 | 强调结果是必然的、直接的 |
| As a result | 中性 | 通用表达，使用频率高 |
| For this reason | 中性 | 解释原因后常用 |
| Due to this | 中性 | 指代前文原因，较口语化 |
| Accordingly | 正式 | 强调后续行动与前因相符 |

#### Time（时间顺序）

| 衔接词 | 语域 | 用法说明 |
|---|---|---|
| First / First of all | 中性 | 开始序列 |
| Subsequently | 正式 | "随后"，比 then 更正式 |
| Following this | 中性 | 表紧接其后的步骤 |
| Meanwhile | 中性 | 同时发生的两件事 |
| Eventually | 中性 | 最终，经过一段时间后 |
| Prior to | 正式 | "在……之前"，后接名词 |
| At the same time | 中性 | 同时，也可表转折（"即使如此"） |

#### Example（举例说明）

| 衔接词 | 语域 | 用法说明 |
|---|---|---|
| For example | 中性 | 最通用的举例，可放句首或句中 |
| For instance | 中性 | 与 for example 同义，稍更口语 |
| Such as | 中性 | 后接名词列举，不能引导完整句 |
| Namely | 正式 | "即"，后面是对前文的具体说明 |
| To illustrate | 正式 | 用例子来阐明，常用于学术 |
| In particular | 正式 | "尤其"，突出特定例子 |
| Specifically | 中性 | 更具体地说明前文 |

#### Emphasis（强调）

| 衔接词 | 语域 | 用法说明 |
|---|---|---|
| Indeed | 正式 | 加强语气，确认前文 |
| In fact | 中性 | 引出更准确的信息或出人意料的事实 |
| Certainly | 中性 | 表确定性 |
| Above all | 中性 | "最重要的是" |
| Particularly | 中性 | 强调特别突出的一点 |
| Especially | 中性 | 用法与 particularly 相近 |
| Crucially | 正式 | 强调关键性，学术写作常用 |
| Significantly | 正式 | "重要的是"，引出重要观点 |

### 衔接的三种语言手段

除了衔接词，还有以下三种重要的衔接手段：

#### ① 代词指代（Pronoun Reference）

用代词（it, they, this, these, that, those, which）指代前文提到的名词：

```
The smartphone has transformed modern communication. It has reduced
face-to-face interactions, altered attention spans, and reshaped
social norms. These changes are particularly visible among teenagers,
who spend an average of seven hours per day on their devices.
```

注意：代词指代必须清晰，避免歧义（antecedent ambiguity）。

**错误示例**：`John told Mark that he had failed. (who failed?)`
**正确示例**：`John told Mark, "You have failed."`

#### ② 词汇重复与同义替换（Lexical Repetition & Synonymy）

适度重复关键词，并用同义词或上下义词进行替换，既保持连贯又避免单调：

```
Climate change poses existential threats to coastal communities.
This environmental crisis — driven by decades of carbon emissions —
is accelerating at a rate that surpasses even the most pessimistic
scientific projections. The warming phenomenon shows no signs of
abating without dramatic policy intervention.
```

在这个段落中：
- `climate change` → `this environmental crisis` → `the warming phenomenon`（同义替换链）

#### ③ 平行结构（Parallel Structure）

相似的语法结构在视觉和语义上产生连贯感：

```
The new policy will reduce carbon emissions, create green jobs,
and stimulate sustainable economic growth.
                    ↑ 三个动词短语形成平行
```

## 21.6 段落间的连贯（Coherence）

如果说衔接（Cohesion）是段落内部句子之间的粘合剂，那么**连贯（Coherence）** 就是段落之间的逻辑纽带。一篇文章的连贯性来自于段落之间清晰的逻辑关系。

### 段落之间的六种逻辑关系

| 逻辑关系 | 英文 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| 时间顺序 | Chronological | 按时间先后组织段落 |
| 空间顺序 | Spatial | 按位置关系组织（由近到远等） |
| 递进关系 | Progressive | 从简单到复杂，从次要到重要 |
| 对比关系 | Contrastive | 交替展示两方面 |
| 因果关系 | Causal | 先因后果，或先果后因 |
| 问题-解决 | Problem-Solution | 提出问题，再给出方案 |

### 过渡段与过渡句

**过渡句（Transition Sentence）** 是一个段落的结尾句或下一段的开头句，其功能是向读者揭示段落之间的逻辑关系。

**类型一：总结上段 + 预告下段（最推荐）**

```
Having established that social media addiction is neurologically
real and measurable, we must now turn to the more challenging
question of how educational institutions can respond effectively.
```

**类型二：纯预告型**

```
Yet economic factors alone cannot account for the full scope of
the problem; cultural attitudes toward mental health also play a decisive role.
```

**类型三：独立过渡段**

在长篇论文中，有时用一整个短段落（2-3句）来标记从一个主要部分到另一个部分的过渡：

```
The evidence reviewed in the preceding section demonstrates
that the current healthcare system is failing vulnerable populations
on multiple fronts. The following section examines three policy
interventions that have shown measurable success in comparable
national contexts.
```

## 21.7 常见段落问题诊断

### 问题一：缺乏主题句

**症状**：段落开头直接进入细节，读者不知道这段在讲什么。

**修改前（有问题）**：
```
In 2019, Netflix raised its subscription price by $2.00. Disney+
launched at $6.99 per month. Hulu added ads to its free tier.
Amazon Prime increased annual fees by 17%.
```

**修改后（有主题句）**：
```
The streaming wars of 2019 were characterized not by content
innovation, but by a race to monetize existing subscriber bases
more aggressively. Netflix raised its subscription price by $2.00.
Disney+ launched with a deceptively low $6.99 entry point designed
for market penetration. Hulu added ads to its free tier, effectively
downgrading the user experience, while Amazon Prime increased its
annual fee by 17%.
```

### 问题二：偏离主题（Off-topic Sentences）

**症状**：段落中出现与主题句无关的信息。

**修改前（有问题）**：
```
Regular exercise has profound mental health benefits. Aerobic
activity triggers the release of endorphins, which reduce stress
and improve mood. Many gyms now offer specialized mental wellness
classes. Physical activity also improves sleep quality, which is
closely linked to emotional regulation. The fitness industry
generated $96 billion in revenue last year. Studies show that
just 30 minutes of brisk walking three times per week can reduce
symptoms of depression by up to 40%.
```

（"The fitness industry generated $96 billion..."与精神健康无关，应删除或移至别段）

**修改后**：
```
Regular exercise has profound mental health benefits. Aerobic
activity triggers the release of endorphins, which reduce stress
and improve mood. Physical activity also improves sleep quality,
which is closely linked to emotional regulation. Indeed, studies
show that just 30 minutes of brisk walking three times per week
can reduce symptoms of depression by up to 40%.
```

### 问题三：支撑不足（Underdevelopment）

**症状**：主题句提出后，仅用1-2句话笼统重复，没有实质性展开。

**修改前（有问题）**：
```
Childhood poverty has long-lasting effects on adult outcomes.
Poor children often struggle more in life. It is a serious problem
that society should address.
```

**修改后（充分展开）**：
```
Childhood poverty leaves deep and lasting imprints on adult
health and economic outcomes. A landmark 30-year longitudinal
study by Harvard researchers found that children raised below
the poverty line were 3.5 times more likely to develop chronic
illnesses such as diabetes and heart disease by age 40. Their
educational attainment averaged two full years less than peers
from middle-income families, directly suppressing lifetime
earning potential. Perhaps most strikingly, the neurological
effects of early stress — elevated cortisol levels stunting
hippocampal development — have been shown to impair working
memory and emotional regulation well into adulthood, creating
cycles of disadvantage that span generations.
```

### 问题四：衔接断裂（Disjointed Flow）

**症状**：段落读起来像是一列孤立的事实，句子之间没有逻辑连接。

**修改前（断裂）**：
```
The Amazon rainforest is shrinking. Brazil's deforestation rate
increased 22% in 2021. Indigenous communities are displaced.
Biodiversity is being destroyed. Carbon emissions increase.
Climate change accelerates.
```

**修改后（流畅衔接）**：
```
The Amazon rainforest is disappearing at an alarming rate: Brazil's
deforestation increased by 22% in 2021 alone. As a direct consequence,
indigenous communities that have stewarded these lands for millennia
are being violently displaced. Beyond the human cost, the destruction
of biodiversity is irreversible — countless species are lost before
they can even be catalogued by science. Moreover, as the forest
vanishes, its capacity to absorb carbon dioxide diminishes, in turn
accelerating the very climate change that makes tropical agriculture
more precarious and deforestation economically tempting. The cycle,
in short, is self-reinforcing and catastrophic.
```

## 本章小结

| 核心概念 | 要点 |
|---|---|
| 段落原则 | 一段一思想；由主题句、支撑句、结论句构成 |
| 主题句 | 明确、可控、有方向；通常位于段首 |
| 六种展开方法 | 举例、解释、数据、对比、因果、引用 |
| 结论句 | 重述/总结/引含义/衔接下段；不引入新内容 |
| 衔接（Cohesion） | 衔接词 + 代词指代 + 词汇替换 + 平行结构 |
| 连贯（Coherence） | 段落间的逻辑关系 + 过渡句/过渡段 |
| 常见问题 | 缺主题句、偏题、展开不足、衔接断裂 |

写好段落的核心在于**纪律性**：每段只说一件事，说清楚，说完整，然后前进。这种纪律性不是对创造力的约束，而是让创造力得以有效传达的框架。
