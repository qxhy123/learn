# 第8章：介词、连词与感叹词——关系、连接与情感的语言编码

> "Prepositions are not just words—they are cognitive maps of how we perceive relationships in space and time."
> —George Lakoff（改述）
>
> 介词被称为英语学习的"最后一公里"——即便英语说得流利的人，也会在介词搭配上偶尔出错。这是因为介词不只是语法工具，它们编码了人类对空间、时间和抽象关系的认知方式。本章将用认知语言学视角来解构介词，让你从"死记硬背"转向"理解规律"。

---

## 8.1 介词的核心概念：空间隐喻理论

### 8.1.1 什么是空间隐喻（Spatial Metaphor）

认知语言学（Cognitive Linguistics）的核心发现之一是：**所有抽象的介词用法，都源于其基本的空间含义**。当我们说 "in trouble"（处于麻烦中），我们在用空间概念（in = 里面）来隐喻抽象处境；当我们说 "under pressure"（承受压力），我们在用物理方向（under = 下方）来描述心理状态。

```
空间含义 → 时间含义 → 抽象含义（依次扩展）

in（在...里面）：
    空间：  She is in the room.          （她在房间里）
    时间：  in the morning, in 2024      （在某段时间内）
    抽象：  in trouble, in love, in debt  （处于某种状态中）

on（在...上面/接触面）：
    空间：  The book is on the table.    （书在桌子上）
    时间：  on Monday, on March 15th     （在某个具体时间点）
    抽象：  on sale, on duty, on strike   （处于某种运作状态）

at（在某个点）：
    空间：  She is at the door.          （她在门口）
    时间：  at noon, at 3 o'clock        （在某个具体时刻）
    抽象：  at risk, at work, at war      （处于某个状态节点）
```

### 8.1.2 介词的意义网络

每个介词都有一个以基本空间含义为中心的**语义网络**（Semantic Network），理解这个网络比死记每个搭配更有效：

```
IN 的意义网络：
核心：封闭空间内部
├── 容器中：water in a glass, books in a bag
├── 地理区域内：in China, in the city, in the street（英式）
├── 时间段内：in the morning, in summer, in 2024
├── 状态中：in pain, in debt, in love, in danger
├── 方式/手段：in English, in writing, in cash
└── 顺序/序列：in order, in turn, in alphabetical order
```

## 8.2 时间介词详解

时间介词是使用频率最高、最容易出错的介词类别。

### 8.2.1 at / on / in 时间用法的三层结构

```
AT（点）→ 最小时间单位，精确时刻
    at 6 o'clock / at noon / at midnight / at dawn
    at the moment / at that time / at the same time
    at the beginning / at the end / at the start
    at Christmas / at Easter / at the weekend（英式）

    固定搭配：
    at night（晚上，不是 in the night）
    at the age of（在...岁时）：She started school at the age of 6.

ON（面）→ 具体日期/特定某天
    on Monday / on weekdays / on weekends（美式）
    on March 15th / on my birthday / on New Year's Day
    on that day / on the following morning / on a cold night
    on time（准时，表示按计划）vs. in time（及时，来得及）

IN（段）→ 较长时间段
    in the morning / in the afternoon / in the evening
    in January / in summer / in 2024 / in the 21st century
    in time（及时）vs. on time（准时）
    in the end（最终）vs. at the end（在结尾处）
```

### 8.2.2 易混淆的时间介词

```
BY vs. UNTIL（TILL）

by（截止，强调"不迟于某时"，通常为一次性动作）：
    Please finish this by Friday.  （周五之前完成）
    She will be back by 6 pm.      （6点前回来）
    By the time he arrived, she had left.

until/till（持续直到，强调动作/状态持续到某时间点）：
    I waited until midnight.       （我等到午夜）
    She stayed in bed until noon.  （她待在床上直到中午）
    Don't leave until I come back.

比较：
    I'll work by Friday.   （我在周五前完成工作——结果）
    I'll work until Friday.（我工作到周五——持续过程）

SINCE vs. FOR

since（从某个时间点开始，用完成时）：
    She has lived here since 2010.   （自2010年起）
    I haven't seen him since Monday.
    She's been waiting since 9 am.

for（持续了多长时间，可与多种时态连用）：
    She has lived here for ten years. （持续了10年）
    I waited for two hours.           （我等了两个小时）
    He studied for the exam all night.

辨别技巧：since 后接时间点，for 后接时间段
    since 2010 / since last week / since I was born
    for ten years / for a long time / for three days

IN vs. WITHIN vs. AFTER

in（从现在起一段时间后）：
    I'll be ready in ten minutes.   （10分钟后）
    She'll arrive in an hour.

within（在...之内，强调不超过该时间）：
    Please reply within 24 hours.  （24小时内回复）
    The report is due within a week.

after（在某时间点之后，不强调起点）：
    I'll call you after lunch.
    After three days, he recovered.
```

### 8.2.3 其他时间介词

```
during（在...期间，与特定时间段连用）：
    during the meeting / during summer / during the war
    They talked during the film.
    （注意：during ≠ for；during 接特定时间段，for 接时间长度）

before / after（之前 / 之后）：
    before dinner / after the exam
    Call me before 5 pm.

from...to.../from...until（从...到...）：
    from Monday to Friday
    from 9 am until 5 pm

ago（过去...以前，接过去时）：
    three days ago / two years ago / a long time ago
    She left an hour ago.
    （ago 与 before 的区别：ago 以现在为参照，before 以过去某点为参照）
    She left an hour ago.         （以现在为参照）
    She had left an hour before.  （以过去某时为参照）
```

## 8.3 地点介词详解

### 8.3.1 at / on / in 地点用法

```
AT（点）→ 精确地点，强调位置
    at the bus stop / at the corner / at the traffic lights
    at school / at work / at home / at the office
    at the top / at the bottom / at the side

    注意：at 表示地址门牌号：
    She lives at 42 Baker Street.（门牌号用 at）
    She lives in Baker Street.（街道名用 in）
    She lives in London.（城市/国家用 in）

ON（面）→ 表面接触
    on the table / on the wall / on the ceiling / on the floor
    on the road / on the street（美式，在街道上）
    on the left / on the right / on the coast / on the border
    on page 5 / on the screen / on TV / on the radio

IN（体）→ 三维空间内部
    in the box / in the bag / in the room / in the building
    in the street（英式）/ in the park / in the garden
    in the water / in the sky / in the world
    in the north / in the east（方位区域用 in）
```

### 8.3.2 空间关系介词详解

```
ABOVE vs. OVER（上方）

above：高于某物，但不一定直接在正上方，也不涉及"覆盖"
    The plane flew above the clouds.   （飞机在云层上方飞行）
    The temperature is above zero.     （温度在零度以上）
    Her score is above average.        （她的成绩在平均分以上）

over：直接在正上方，或"覆盖"，或"越过"
    There's a lamp over the table.     （灯在桌子正上方）
    She spread a blanket over the bed. （盖上毯子）
    He jumped over the fence.          （跳过栅栏）
    over 100 people（超过，= more than）

BELOW vs. UNDER（下方）

below：低于某物（不一定直接在正下方），抽象使用也多
    The temperature fell below freezing.
    Her score is below average.
    below sea level / below the surface

under：直接在正下方，或"覆盖之下"，抽象意义丰富
    The cat is under the table.
    under the bed / under the bridge
    under pressure（承受压力）under control（受控制）
    under construction（在建设中）under discussion（在讨论中）
    under 18（未满18岁，= less than）

BETWEEN vs. AMONG

between：两者之间（两个独立个体之间）
    She sat between Tom and Mary.
    between the two buildings
    between you and me（私下里）
    between 9 and 10 o'clock

among：三者或多者之间（群体内部，成员难以一一列举）
    She is the most talented among her classmates.
    among the trees / among the crowd
    a feeling of trust among team members

    注意：between 也可用于三个以上明确个体：
    a treaty between France, Germany and Italy ✓
    （三个具体国家之间，可用 between）

BESIDE vs. BESIDES

beside（介词）：在...旁边（空间位置）
    She sat beside me.
    The hotel is beside the river.

besides（介词/副词）：除了...之外（还），而且
    Besides English, she speaks French.   （除了英语，她还会法语）
    The movie was long; besides, it was boring. （此外）
    Who else is coming, besides Tom?      （除了 Tom 还有谁？）
```

## 8.4 抽象介词与固定搭配

### 8.4.1 方式、工具、伴随介词

```
BY（方式/手段）：
    by car / by bus / by train / by plane（交通工具，无冠词）
    by hand（手工）/ by machine / by post / by phone / by email
    by accident / by chance / by mistake（偶然/错误地）
    by oneself（独自）
    multiply by 3（乘以3）/ divide by 4（除以4）

WITH（工具/伴随/状态）：
    write with a pen（用钢笔写）
    She came with her sister.（和她姐姐一起）
    The man with gray hair（那个白发男人）
    trembling with fear（因恐惧而颤抖）
    filled with hope（充满希望）

WITHOUT（没有/缺乏）：
    without permission / without hesitation
    She left without saying goodbye.
    We can't succeed without effort.

IN（方式/语言/状态）：
    in English / in Chinese / in writing
    in a hurry / in a rush / in silence / in secret
    speak in a whisper（低声说）
```

### 8.4.2 重要的固定介词搭配

**形容词 + 介词（Adjective + Preposition）：**

```
+ of：
    afraid of, aware of, capable of, fond of, proud of,
    ashamed of, guilty of, jealous of, scared of, tired of,
    full of, short of, typical of, worthy of

+ at：
    good at, bad at, skilled at, surprised at, amazed at,
    angry at/with, aimed at, alarmed at

+ in：
    interested in, experienced in, involved in,
    rich in, poor in, weak in, successful in

+ with：
    angry with/at, bored with, content with, pleased with,
    satisfied with, familiar with, popular with, associated with

+ to：
    accustomed to, addicted to, allergic to, committed to,
    dedicated to, devoted to, opposed to, used to, married to,
    related to, similar to, sensitive to

+ for：
    responsible for, famous for, known for, ready for,
    grateful for, sorry for, suitable for, eligible for
```

**动词 + 介词（Verb + Preposition）：**

```
+ of：
    consist of（由...组成）, approve of, remind of,
    accuse of, suspect of, boast of, deprive of

+ in：
    believe in, participate in, result in（导致）,
    engage in, invest in, specialize in

+ on：
    depend on/upon, rely on, insist on, concentrate on,
    focus on, comment on, congratulate on

+ for：
    apply for, ask for, pay for, search for, wait for,
    apologize for, blame for, thank for, vote for

+ with：
    agree with, deal with, cope with, interfere with,
    provide with, supply with, compare with/to

+ to：
    belong to, contribute to, lead to, refer to,
    respond to, subscribe to, attend to, listen to

+ from：
    suffer from, benefit from, result from（源于）,
    differ from, escape from, recover from, protect from
```

## 8.5 并列连词（FANBOYS）

并列连词（Coordinating Conjunctions）连接语法地位相等的成分（词与词、短语与短语、句子与句子）。英语并列连词共7个，用首字母组成助记词 **FANBOYS**：

```
F - For      （因为，解释原因，较正式）
A - And      （和，而且，然后）
N - Nor      （也不，用于否定并列）
B - But      （但是，然而）
O - Or       （或者，否则）
Y - Yet      （然而，尽管如此）
S - So       （所以，因此）
```

### 8.5.1 各并列连词详解

```
AND（顺接）：
    She is intelligent and hardworking.
    He woke up, had breakfast, and left for work.
    It was cold and raining.

BUT（转折）：
    It was expensive but worth it.
    She tried hard but failed.
    I would help, but I'm too busy.

OR（选择/否则）：
    Would you like tea or coffee?
    Hurry up, or you'll miss the train.（否则）
    You can stay or leave—it's up to you.

NOR（否定并列，前面通常有 neither 或 not）：
    She doesn't smoke, nor does she drink.（倒装！）
    He can't read, nor can he write.
    Neither Tom nor Mary was there.

FOR（原因，较正式，相当于 because 但不常用）：
    He must be tired, for he has been working all day.
    She was happy, for she had passed the exam.

YET（转折，语气比 but 强，暗含意外）：
    It was a small victory, yet it felt significant.
    She is young, yet very mature for her age.

SO（结果/目的）：
    It was raining, so we stayed inside.
    He was tired, so he went to bed early.
    We had no food, so we went to the supermarket.
```

### 8.5.2 并列连词的标点规则

```
连接两个独立句子：用逗号 + 连词
    ✓  She was tired, but she kept working.
    ❌ She was tired but she kept working.（正式写作中逗号不可少）
    ❌ She was tired, she kept working.（逗号拼接错误 Comma Splice）

连接短语/单词：通常不加逗号
    tea and coffee（不加逗号）
    fast but reliable

三个或以上并列：连词加在最后一个前，Oxford Comma 在倒数第二个后
    美式：I bought eggs, milk, and bread.（Oxford Comma，推荐）
    英式：I bought eggs, milk and bread.（不加 Oxford Comma）
```

## 8.6 从属连词（Subordinating Conjunctions）

从属连词引导从句，使从句依附于主句，表达时间、原因、条件、让步、目的、结果等关系。

### 8.6.1 时间连词

```
when（当...时候）：点时间或一般时间
    When she arrived, everyone cheered.
    When I was young, I loved drawing.

while（在...期间，两个动作同时进行）：持续时间段
    While he was cooking, she was reading.
    I fell asleep while watching TV.
    While I agree with the goal, I question the method.（让步，同时含义）

as（随着，当...时）：动态变化，同步进行
    As she grew older, she became wiser.
    As I walked, I listened to music.

before / after（在...之前/之后）：
    Finish your homework before you watch TV.
    After she graduated, she moved to London.

since（自从，用完成时）：
    I've been happy since I met her.
    Since he moved away, we've rarely talked.

until / till（直到...才）：
    Wait here until I return.
    She didn't speak until spoken to.

as soon as（一...就...）：
    As soon as she heard the news, she called me.
    I'll tell you as soon as I know.

once（一旦）：
    Once you understand the rule, it's easy.
    Once the meeting starts, no phones.

by the time（到...时候，用完成时）：
    By the time he arrived, she had already left.
```

### 8.6.2 原因连词

```
because（因为）：最直接，语气最强，可放句首或句中
    She was late because her car broke down.
    Because it was raining, we stayed in.

since / as（既然/因为）：原因已知，语气较弱
    Since you're here, you might as well help.
    As it was getting late, we decided to leave.
    （注意：as 引导原因时通常放句首，避免歧义）

for（因为，并列连词中也有此用）：书面语，放主句后
    She looked happy, for she had good news.

now that（既然...了，含时间变化）：
    Now that she has her degree, she can apply.
    Now that winter is over, the days are getting longer.

in that（在于，正式书面）：
    The plan is flawed in that it ignores costs.
```

### 8.6.3 条件连词

```
if（如果）：最通用的条件连词
    If it rains, we'll stay inside.
    If I were you, I wouldn't do that.

unless（除非，= if...not）：
    Unless you hurry, you'll be late.
    I won't go unless you come with me.
    = I won't go if you don't come with me.

provided / providing (that)（前提是，条件是）：
    You can borrow my car, provided (that) you return it tomorrow.

as long as / so long as（只要）：
    As long as you try your best, I'll be proud.
    So long as you're safe, I don't mind.

in case（以防，对应 just in case）：
    Take an umbrella in case it rains.
    I saved the file in case the computer crashed.
    （in case ≠ if；in case 强调预防）

on condition that（条件是，较正式）：
    I'll help on condition that you keep this secret.

whether (or not)（不管是否）：
    Whether or not you agree, this is the final decision.
```

### 8.6.4 让步连词

```
although / though / even though（虽然，尽管）：
    Although it was cold, they went swimming.
    Though she was tired, she kept smiling.
    Even though I disagree, I'll support the team.
    （语气：even though > although > though）
    （though 还可用在句末：She kept smiling, though.）

while / whilst（尽管，同时含"对比"）：
    While I understand your point, I disagree.
    Whilst the idea is good, the execution is poor.（英式）

even if（即使，强调假设性）：
    Even if I fail, I won't give up.
    Even if she apologizes, I won't forgive her easily.

despite / in spite of（尽管，后接名词/动名词，非从句！）：
    Despite the rain, they continued playing.
    In spite of his illness, he came to work.
    ❌ Despite he was tired, he worked on.（despite 不接从句）
    ✓  Although he was tired, he worked on.（接从句用 although）
```

### 8.6.5 目的与结果连词

```
目的连词：
so that / in order that（为了，以便）：
    She studied hard so that she could pass.
    I saved money in order that I could travel.

    简化形式（不定式代替）：
    She studied hard (in order) to pass.

for fear (that) / in case（担心，以防）：
    She whispered for fear (that) she might wake him.

结果连词：
so...that（如此...以至于）：
    He was so tired that he fell asleep immediately.
    The music was so loud that we couldn't talk.

such...that（如此...以至于，接名词短语）：
    It was such a good film that we watched it twice.
    She has such talent that everyone admires her.
```

## 8.7 关联连词（Correlative Conjunctions）

关联连词成对出现，连接两个平行的成分，**要求严格的平行结构（Parallelism）**。

```
both...and（两者都）：
    Both Tom and Mary passed the exam.
    She is both intelligent and hardworking.
    ❌ She is both intelligent and works hard.（结构不平行）
    ✓  She is both intelligent and diligent.（两个形容词，平行）

either...or（要么...要么，两选一）：
    Either you come with me, or you stay here.
    The answer is either yes or no.
    动词单复数：取决于靠近动词的主语
    Either he or his brothers are responsible.（brothers 靠近动词）
    Either his brothers or he is responsible.

neither...nor（既不...也不）：
    She speaks neither French nor German.
    Neither the manager nor the employees were informed.
    ❌ Neither he agrees nor she agrees.（须平行）
    ✓  Neither he nor she agrees.

not only...but also（不仅...而且）：
    She is not only talented but also dedicated.
    Not only did he apologize, but he also offered to help.
    （注意：Not only 位于句首时，主句倒装！）
    ❌ Not only he apologized but he also helped.
    ✓  Not only did he apologize, but he also helped.

not...but（不是...而是）：
    It is not the destination but the journey that matters.

whether...or（不管...还是）：
    Whether you succeed or fail, don't give up.

as...as（和...一样）：
    She is as talented as her sister.

the...the（越...越...，搭配比较级）：
    The harder you work, the better the results.
    The more you give, the more you receive.
```

## 8.8 感叹词与话语标记

### 8.8.1 感叹词（Interjections）

感叹词是表达情感、态度或反应的词语，通常独立使用，不与句子产生语法关系。

```
表达惊讶：
    Oh! / Wow! / Gosh! / Good heavens! / My goodness!
    Wow, that's amazing!    Oh, I didn't know that.

表达高兴/认可：
    Great! / Wonderful! / Fantastic! / Excellent! / Brilliant!
    Yeah! / Yay! / Hooray! / Right on!

表达痛苦/失望：
    Ouch! / Ow!（疼痛）    Oh no! / Damn! / Blast!（失望/沮丧）
    Ugh!（厌恶）    Boo!（嘘声/失望）

表达犹豫/思考：
    Um... / Uh... / Er... / Hmm...
    Well, let me think...    Actually, I'm not sure.

表达感谢/礼貌：
    Thanks! / Thank you! / Cheers!（英式，多功能）
    Please. / Excuse me. / Sorry!
```

### 8.8.2 话语标记（Discourse Markers）

话语标记（Discourse Markers）是管理对话流程、表达说话者态度的功能词或短语，不改变命题内容，但对交际至关重要。

**组织话语（Organizing Discourse）：**

```
顺序标记：
    First(ly) / To begin with / Initially...
    Second(ly) / Then / Next / After that...
    Finally / Lastly / To conclude...

增加信息：
    Also / In addition / Furthermore / Moreover / Besides
    What's more / On top of that / Not only that

对比转折：
    However / Nevertheless / Nonetheless
    On the other hand / In contrast / That said / Even so
    But / Yet / Still / Then again / At the same time

举例说明：
    For example / For instance / Such as / Like
    To illustrate / A case in point is...

解释澄清：
    In other words / That is to say / I mean / Namely
    What I'm trying to say is... / To put it simply...

总结：
    In conclusion / To sum up / In short / In brief
    All in all / On the whole / To summarize
```

**表达说话者态度（Stance Markers）：**

```
坦白/诚实：
    Honestly / Frankly / To be honest / To tell the truth
    If I'm honest... / To be frank...

显然/肯定：
    Obviously / Clearly / Evidently / Apparently / Surely
    Needless to say / It goes without saying that...

表示意外：
    Surprisingly / Interestingly / Remarkably / Curiously
    As it turns out... / It turns out that...

语气缓和（Hedging，软化表达）：
    Perhaps / Maybe / Possibly / Probably
    I think / I believe / I suppose / I guess / I feel
    It seems (that) / It appears (that) / Apparently
    In my opinion / From my perspective / As far as I know
```

**口语中的话语标记（Conversational Markers）：**

```
开始话轮（Turn-taking）：
    Well, ... / Right, ... / So, ... / OK, ...
    The thing is... / The point is... / The fact is...

填充词（Fillers，思考时使用，不宜过多）：
    you know, like, I mean, sort of, kind of, basically,
    actually, right, literally, totally

    ⚠️ 过度使用 like 和 you know 是口语不成熟的标志！

确认理解：
    Right? / OK? / You see? / Don't you think? / Isn't it?
    You know what I mean? / Do you follow me?

纠正/重述：
    Actually, ... / In fact, ... / As a matter of fact,...
    What I mean is... / Let me put it another way...
```

## 8.9 十大词性总结与词性转换规律

### 8.9.1 英语十大词性总表

| 词性 | 英文名称 | 功能 | 典型例词 |
|------|---------|------|----------|
| 名词（N） | Noun | 命名人/事/物/概念 | book, freedom, Beijing |
| 代词（Pron） | Pronoun | 代替名词 | he, they, this, who |
| 形容词（Adj） | Adjective | 修饰名词/作表语 | beautiful, important, red |
| 动词（V） | Verb | 表示动作/状态 | run, be, think, seem |
| 副词（Adv） | Adverb | 修饰动词/形容词/副词/句子 | quickly, very, fortunately |
| 介词（Prep） | Preposition | 表示名词与其他词的关系 | in, on, at, for, with |
| 连词（Conj） | Conjunction | 连接词、短语、句子 | and, but, because, although |
| 冠词（Art） | Article | 限定名词 | a, an, the |
| 数词（Num） | Numeral | 表示数量/顺序 | one, first, twice |
| 感叹词（Interj） | Interjection | 表达情感/反应 | Oh! Wow! Well... |

### 8.9.2 词性转换规律（Word Formation）

英语通过前缀（Prefix）和后缀（Suffix）进行词性转换，掌握这些规律可以大幅扩大词汇量。

**名词化后缀（形成名词）：**

```
动词 → 名词：
    -tion / -sion：  create → creation, decide → decision
    -ment：          develop → development, achieve → achievement
    -ance / -ence：  perform → performance, prefer → preference
    -al：            arrive → arrival, refuse → refusal
    -er / -or：      teach → teacher, act → actor
    -ing：           meet → meeting, feel → feeling（动名词）

形容词 → 名词：
    -ness：          happy → happiness, kind → kindness
    -ity / -ty：     real → reality, cruel → cruelty
    -cy：            efficient → efficiency, accurate → accuracy
    -ism：           real → realism, national → nationalism

范例词族（Word Family）：
    educate（V） → education（N） → educational（Adj） → educationally（Adv）
    → educator（N） → educated（Adj）

    create（V） → creation（N） → creative（Adj） → creatively（Adv）
    → creator（N） → creativity（N）
```

**形容词化后缀（形成形容词）：**

```
名词 → 形容词：
    -ful：           hope → hopeful, beauty → beautiful
    -less：          hope → hopeless, care → careless（反义）
    -ous / -ious：   danger → dangerous, glory → glorious
    -al：            nation → national, origin → original
    -ic / -ical：    hero → heroic, history → historical
    -ive：           act → active, create → creative
    -able / -ible：  read → readable, sense → sensible
    -y：             rain → rainy, wind → windy, health → healthy
    -ish：           child → childish, fool → foolish

动词 → 形容词：
    -ing（主动）：    an interesting book, a tiring journey
    -ed（被动）：     an interested student, a tired teacher
```

**副词化后缀：**

```
形容词 → 副词：
    大多数：+ -ly：  quick → quickly, beautiful → beautifully
    
    特殊变化：
    -y 结尾：y → ily：happy → happily, easy → easily
    -le 结尾：le → ly：simple → simply, gentle → gently
    -ic 结尾：+ ally：basic → basically, dramatic → dramatically
    例外：public → publicly（不是 publically）
```

### 8.9.3 常见词根与意义

```
拉丁词根：
    -port-（搬运）：import, export, transport, portable, report
    -dict-（说）：  predict, dictate, verdict, diction, contradict
    -rupt-（破裂）：interrupt, corrupt, disrupt, bankrupt, erupt
    -vis-/-vid-（看）：vision, visual, evident, provide, revise

希腊词根：
    -graph-（写）：photograph, biography, autograph, geography
    -log-（词/学）：biology, psychology, dialogue, catalogue
    -path-（感受/病）：sympathy, empathy, pathology, telepathy
    -phon-（声音）：phone, microphone, symphony, phonetics
```

### 练习二：地点介词辨析

选择正确的介词：

1. The painting is hanging (on / in / at) the wall.
2. She lives (on / in / at) 25 Oxford Street.
3. The temperature is (below / under) average for this time of year.
4. There are clouds (above / over) the mountains.
5. Choose (between / among) the three options.

### 练习三：从属连词选择

选择正确的从属连词：

1. ___ (Although / Despite) the heavy rain, the match continued.
2. She prepared thoroughly ___ (so that / in order) she could present confidently.
3. ___ (Even if / Even though) you don't like it, you must eat it.（事实上不喜欢）
4. Take your phone ___ (in case / if) you need to call me.
5. ___ (While / Though) I respect your view, I disagree.
6. ___ (Unless / If) you hurry, you'll catch the train.（暗含"你不会赶上"）

### 练习四：关联连词与平行结构

改正下列句子中的平行结构错误：

1. She is both talented and works very hard.

3. Either you apologize or leaving now.
4. She neither apologized nor was explaining herself.

### 练习五：词性转换

按要求转换词性：

1. educate（V）→ ___ (N，过程）→ ___(Adj)→ ___(Adv)
2. beauty（N）→ ___ (Adj) → ___(V) → ___(Adv)
3. depend（V）→ ___(N，依赖）→ ___(Adj，独立的，反义）

**参考答案：**

练习二：1. on  2. at  3. below  4. above  5. among（三个选项中选一个）/ between（明确是三个时也可）6. beside

练习三：1. Despite（后接名词短语）/ Although（后接从句，如改为 Although it rained heavily）  2. so that  3. Even though（已知事实）  4. in case  5. While  6. Unless

## 本章小结

| 知识点 | 核心规律 | 记忆要点 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 介词空间隐喻 | 所有介词源于空间概念，再延伸至时间和抽象意义 | at=点，on=面，in=体（空间→时间→抽象） |
| 时间介词 | at（时刻）, on（日期）, in（时段）；by（截止）vs. until（持续） | since+时间点 / for+时间段 |
| 地点介词 | at（位置点）, on（接触面）, in（内部空间） | above/below（不接触）vs. over/under（正上下方或覆盖） |
| 并列连词 | FANBOYS 7个；连接独立句时需加逗号 | Comma splice 是严重写作错误 |
| 从属连词 | 时间/原因/条件/让步/目的/结果各有一组 | despite/in spite of 接名词，不接从句 |
| 关联连词 | 成对出现，要求严格平行结构 | Not only...but also 句首时主句倒装 |
| 感叹词 | 表达情感，不影响句法 | 避免过度使用口语填充词 |
| 词性转换 | 前缀/后缀规律可预测和记忆新词汇 | 掌握词根可以举一反三 |

## 第二部分总结：词性精讲综合回顾

学完第5至第8章，我们已经系统梳理了英语的十大词性。以下是整体视角的回顾：

```
英语句子结构的底层逻辑：

[主语（名词/代词）] + [谓语（动词）] + [宾语/表语（名词/形容词）]
       ↑                    ↑                     ↑
   冠词/形容词修饰        副词修饰            介词短语补充
       ↓                    ↓                     ↓
  连词连接多个主语     连词连接多个谓语         连词连接从句

词性间的互动关系：
名词     ←由→  冠词、形容词修饰；由介词标记关系
动词     ←由→  副词修饰；情态动词限定语气
形容词   ←由→  副词程度词修饰；可作定语、表语、补语
介词     →     后接名词/代词/动名词，构成介词短语
连词     →     连接同类成分或引导从句
```

**第二部分核心学习成果：**
1. 能够正确区分名词的可数性，并选择合适冠词
2. 能够准确使用各类代词，保持代词指代清晰
3. 能够正确排列多个形容词的顺序
4. 能够区分情态动词的语义层次
5. 能够识别并正确使用短语动词
6. 能够掌握副词位置规则，避免常见错位错误
7. 能够区分介词的时间、地点用法，理解固定搭配
8. 能够使用连词构建逻辑清晰的复合句和复杂句

> **展望第三部分：** 词性是砖块，时态是建筑蓝图。下一部分（时态体系）将教你如何用动词的变化形式来精确表达时间关系——这是英语最系统也最迷人的部分。
