# 第6章：代词与形容词——指代的艺术与描述的精度

> "Adjectives are the sugar and salt of language—used sparingly, they enhance; used excessively, they overwhelm."
> —Jan Venolia
>
> 代词是语言的粘合剂，它让我们避免重复，保持表达流畅；形容词则是语言的调色板，赋予名词以色彩和质感。掌握这两类词，你的英语表达将从"能看懂"跃升为"有品质"。

---

## 6.1 人称代词系统

人称代词（Personal Pronouns）是最基础也是最系统的代词类型。英语人称代词按人称（第一/二/三人称）、数（单数/复数）、格（主格/宾格/所有格）以及性（男/女/中性）进行区分。

### 6.1.1 完整人称代词表

| 人称 | 主格（Subject） | 宾格（Object） | 形容词性所有格 | 名词性所有格 | 反身代词（Reflexive） |
|------|----------------|---------------|--------------|------------|----------------------|
| 第一人称单数 | I | me | my | mine | myself |
| 第二人称单数/复数 | you | you | your | yours | yourself / yourselves |
| 第三人称单数（男） | he | him | his | his | himself |
| 第三人称单数（女） | she | her | her | hers | herself |
| 第三人称单数（中） | it | it | its | its | itself |
| 第一人称复数 | we | us | our | ours | ourselves |
| 第三人称复数 | they | them | their | theirs | themselves |

> **注意细节：**
> - `it's` = it is 或 it has（缩写）
> - `its` = 它的（所有格，无撇号）
> - 这是英语中最常见的拼写错误之一！

### 6.1.2 形容词性所有格 vs. 名词性所有格

```
形容词性所有格（Possessive Adjective）：修饰名词，后面必须跟名词
    This is my book.       （my 修饰 book）
    That is her car.
    We love our country.

名词性所有格（Possessive Pronoun）：独立使用，替代"所有格+名词"
    This book is mine.     （mine = my book，独立使用）
    That car is hers.
    This country is ours.

对比使用：
    Your idea is good, but mine is better.
    （mine = my idea，避免重复 idea）

    I forgot my umbrella. Can I borrow yours?
    （yours = your umbrella）
```

### 6.1.3 反身代词的用法

反身代词（Reflexive Pronouns）有两种主要用法：

**用法1：反身用法（动作回指主语）**

```
She hurt herself while cooking.         （她做饭时伤了自己）
He taught himself to play guitar.       （他自学吉他）
They enjoyed themselves at the party.   （他们在聚会上玩得很开心）
I looked at myself in the mirror.
```

**用法2：强调用法（Emphatic Use，可省略不影响句意）**

```
I myself don't believe it.              （我自己也不相信）
The president himself attended.         （总统亲自出席）
She did it (all) by herself.            （她独自完成的）
The machine itself is not broken.
```

**反身代词常见错误（中国学生）：**

```
❌  Please contact myself if you have questions.
✓   Please contact me if you have questions.
    （myself 不能替代 me 作宾语，除非主语是 I）

❌  My friend and myself went to the cinema.
✓   My friend and I went to the cinema.

❌  He gave it to John and myself.
✓   He gave it to John and me.
```

## 6.2 指示代词与不定代词

### 6.2.1 指示代词（Demonstrative Pronouns）

```
          单数          复数
近指：    this          these    （this/these 指近处或刚提到的）
远指：    that          those    （that/those 指远处或之前提到的）

作代词：
    This is my cat.              （这是我的猫）
    That was a great speech.     （那是一次精彩的演讲）
    These are the results.       （这些就是结果）
    Those were the days!         （那时候多好啊！）

作形容词（限定词）：
    this book, that idea, these students, those problems
```

**this vs. that 在语篇中的用法：**

```
前指（Cataphora）：this 引出即将说明的内容
    Remember this: never give up.    （记住这点：永不放弃）

后指（Anaphora）：that 指代刚刚说过的内容
    She was three hours late. That was unacceptable.

口语中的微妙区别：
    This is what I want to say...    （我接下来要说的是...）
    That's what I mean.              （就是那个意思）
```

### 6.2.2 不定代词（Indefinite Pronouns）的系统

英语不定代词以 some-/any-/no-/every- 为词根，与 -one/-body/-thing/-where 组合构成16个核心不定代词：

| 词根 | -one（人，正式） | -body（人，口语） | -thing（事/物） | -where（地点） |
|------|----------------|-----------------|----------------|---------------|
| some- | someone | somebody | something | somewhere |
| any- | anyone | anybody | anything | anywhere |
| no- | no one | nobody | nothing | nowhere |
| every- | everyone | everybody | everything | everywhere |

**some- 系列 vs. any- 系列的核心区别：**

```
some- 系列：用于肯定句，以及期待肯定回答的疑问句
    Someone called you while you were out.
    Would you like something to eat?     （期待肯定回答）
    Can you give me some advice?

any- 系列：用于否定句、疑问句（中性疑问）、条件句
    I didn't see anyone there.
    Did anyone call?                     （中性疑问）
    If anyone asks, tell them I'm busy.
    You can sit anywhere you like.       （任何地方都可以，肯定句中表"任意"）
```

**no- 系列（相当于 not + any-）：**

```
Nobody came to the meeting.
= Anybody didn't come to the meeting.   ❌（英语不能双重否定）

Nothing happened.
= Anything didn't happen.               ❌

I have nothing to say.                  ✓
= I don't have anything to say.         ✓
```

**every- 系列的语法特点（接单数动词！）：**

```
Everyone is here.                       （not "are here"）
Everybody has their own opinion.        （their 用单数性别中立代词）
Everything is ready.
```

## 6.3 关系代词与疑问代词

### 6.3.1 关系代词（Relative Pronouns）详解

关系代词引导定语从句（Relative Clauses），连接主句与从句，同时在从句中充当句子成分。

| 关系代词 | 先行词 | 从句中的功能 |
|---------|-------|------------|
| who | 人 | 主语 |
| whom | 人 | 宾语（正式） |
| whose | 人/物 | 所有格 |
| which | 物/动物/整句话 | 主语或宾语 |
| that | 人/物 | 主语或宾语（限制性从句） |

**who vs. whom 的区分（主格 vs. 宾格）：**

```
判断方法：将从句独立成句，看空格处应填 he/she 还是 him/her
    He/she → 用 who
    Him/her → 用 whom

The man who called you is my uncle.
→ The man called you. （The man = he）→ 用 who ✓

The woman whom you met is my sister.
→ You met the woman. （the woman = her）→ 用 whom ✓

口语中 whom 常被 who 替代：
    Who did you call?       （口语可接受）
    Whom did you call?      （正式书面语）
```

**限制性从句 vs. 非限制性从句（Restrictive vs. Non-restrictive）：**

```
限制性从句（Defining Relative Clause）：
├── 无逗号隔开
├── that / who / which / whom 均可用
├── 功能：限定先行词，缩小范围
└── 示例：
    The student that/who got the highest score won a prize.
    （那个得了最高分的学生——限定是哪个学生）

非限制性从句（Non-defining Relative Clause）：
├── 用逗号隔开
├── 只能用 who / which / whom，不能用 that
├── 功能：补充说明先行词，可删去不影响主句意思
└── 示例：
    My brother, who lives in London, is a lawyer.
    （我哥哥，他住在伦敦——补充信息）
    The Eiffel Tower, which was built in 1889, attracts millions.
```

**whose 的用法（所有格，可指人也可指物）：**

```
The woman whose car was stolen reported it to the police.
（那个车被偷的女人报了警）

A company whose products are reliable will succeed.
（产品可靠的公司会成功）
```

**关系代词 which 指代整个句子：**

```
He arrived three hours late, which annoyed everyone.
（他迟到了三小时，这让所有人都很恼火——which 指代前面整个句子）

She passed the exam, which surprised her teacher.
```

### 6.3.2 疑问代词（Interrogative Pronouns）

```
who     → 问人（主格）：Who called you?
whom    → 问人（宾格，正式）：Whom did you see?
whose   → 问所属：Whose umbrella is this?
what    → 问事物/选择：What happened? What do you want?
which   → 在有限选择中问：Which do you prefer, tea or coffee?

what vs. which 的区别：
    What color do you like?  （颜色范围开放，无限制）
    Which color do you prefer, red or blue?  （在特定选项中选择）

    What do you do?          （你做什么工作？）
    Which job did you choose?（你选了哪份工作？—— 已知几个选项）
```

## 6.4 代词的一致性与指代清晰原则

### 6.4.1 代词与先行词的一致性（Agreement）

```
数的一致：
    Every student should bring their own pencil.     ✓（现代英语接受）
    Every student should bring his or her own pencil. ✓（正式）
    ❌ Every student should bring their own pencils.（pencil 与 every student 矛盾）

性别的一致：
    The manager called to say she would be late.
    （先行词 manager 为女性，用 she）

集合名词的一致：
    The committee has made its decision.  （美式，整体）
    The committee have made their decision. （英式，强调成员）
```

### 6.4.2 指代清晰原则（Clarity of Reference）

代词必须有明确、唯一的先行词，否则会造成歧义：

```
歧义示例：
❌  When Tom met John, he was nervous.
    （he 指 Tom 还是 John？不清楚）
✓   When Tom met John, Tom was nervous.
✓   Tom was nervous when he met John.

❌  I put the report on the desk, and it was messy.
    （it 指报告还是桌子？）
✓   I put the report on the messy desk.
✓   The report I put on the desk was messy.

✓ 正确的代词使用：
    Sarah finished her project early, and she submitted it on time.
    （her → Sarah，it → project，清晰无歧义）
```

### 6.4.3 性别中立代词（Gender-neutral Pronouns）

现代英语中，当先行词性别未知或为非二元性别时，they/them 作为单数中性代词已被广泛接受：

```
传统写法：
    A doctor should take care of his patients. （预设男性，现已过时）

现代写法：
    A doctor should take care of their patients. ✓
    Doctors should take care of their patients. ✓（改成复数也可避免问题）
```

## 6.5 形容词的分类与功能

形容词（Adjective）是修饰名词或代词的词类，英语形容词与汉语形容词有显著不同——英语形容词本身不变形（不加时态、不区分性数）。

### 6.5.1 形容词的三种句法功能

```
1. 定语（Attributive）：直接修饰名词，通常前置
    a beautiful garden       （美丽的花园）
    an interesting book      （有趣的书）
    the tall man             （那个高个子男人）

2. 表语（Predicative）：位于系动词后，描述主语状态
    The garden is beautiful. （花园很美）
    She looks tired.         （她看起来很累）
    The milk tastes sour.    （牛奶尝起来发酸了）

3. 补语（Postpositive / Object Complement）：修饰宾语
    He found the movie boring.    （他发现这部电影很无聊）
    She painted the wall white.   （她把墙刷成白色）
    Keep the door closed.         （保持门关闭）
```

### 6.5.2 只作定语的形容词 vs. 只作表语的形容词

```
只作定语（不能放在系动词后）：
    utter（完全的）：an utter failure  → ❌ The failure is utter.
    mere（仅仅的）：a mere coincidence → ❌ The coincidence is mere.
    main（主要的）：the main point     → ❌ The point is main.
    elder（年长的）：my elder sister  → ❌ My sister is elder.（用 older）
    sheer（纯粹的）：sheer luck        → ❌ The luck is sheer.

只作表语（不能放在名词前）：
    afraid：She is afraid. → ❌ an afraid girl（用 frightened）
    asleep：The baby is asleep. → ❌ an asleep baby（用 sleeping）
    alive：The fish is alive. → ❌ an alive fish（用 living/live）
    alone：He is alone. → ❌ an alone man（用 lonely/solitary）
    awake：She is awake. → ❌ an awake child（用 wide-awake）
    aware：I am aware of it. → ❌ an aware person（这个词只作表语）
```

## 6.6 形容词的比较级与最高级

### 6.6.1 规则变化

```
单音节形容词：加 -er / -est
    tall → taller → tallest
    fast → faster → fastest
    nice → nicer → nicest     （结尾 e，只加 r / st）
    big → bigger → biggest    （CVC 结构，双写末辅音）

双音节形容词（辅音 + y 结尾）：y → ier / iest
    happy → happier → happiest
    busy → busier → busiest
    early → earlier → earliest
    friendly → friendlier → friendliest

多音节形容词（三音节及以上，以及多数双音节）：more / most
    beautiful → more beautiful → most beautiful
    important → more important → most important
    interesting → more interesting → most interesting
    careful → more careful → most careful
    boring → more boring → most boring
```

### 6.6.2 不规则变化

| 原级 | 比较级 | 最高级 |
|------|--------|--------|
| good / well | better | best |
| bad / ill | worse | worst |
| much / many | more | most |
| little | less | least |
| far | farther / further | farthest / furthest |
| old | older / elder | oldest / eldest |
| late | later / latter | latest / last |

> **farther vs. further：**
> - farther：物理距离（The supermarket is farther than I thought.）
> - further：抽象延伸，也可指物理距离（Do you have any further questions? / further down the road）

> **elder vs. older：**
> - elder：家庭成员间的排行（my elder brother，只作定语）
> - older：一般年龄比较，可作定语或表语（She is older than me.）

### 6.6.3 比较结构的重要句型

```
同级比较（as...as）：
    She is as tall as her mother.
    This is not as difficult as I thought.
    He earns twice as much as I do.

比较级 + than：
    Beijing is larger than Shanghai.
    The movie was more interesting than I expected.
    I have fewer friends than she does.（可数名词用 fewer）
    He has less money than me.（不可数名词用 less）

最高级（+ in/of）：
    She is the tallest in her class.         （in + 地点/群体）
    This is the most beautiful of all.       （of + 复数/all）
    He is the best player on the team.

比较级的特殊结构：
    The more you read, the better you write. （越...越...）
    He is getting better and better.         （越来越...）
    This is by far the best option.          （远远是最好的）
    She is no less talented than her sister. （不比...差）
```

### 6.6.4 绝对形容词（Absolute Adjectives）

某些形容词本身含有"极端"含义，理论上不能有比较级/最高级：

```
perfect（完美的），unique（独一无二的），dead（死的），
empty（空的），full（满的），infinite（无限的），
absolute（绝对的），universal（普遍的）

❌ more perfect，❌ most unique（严格语法）
✓  more nearly perfect（更接近完美）
✓  almost unique（几乎独一无二）

注：现代英语口语中 "more unique" 等用法已逐渐被接受，但正式写作仍应避免。
```

## 6.7 形容词排列顺序规则

当多个形容词同时修饰一个名词时，英语有严格的顺序规则。以下是著名的 **OPSHACOM** 顺序（也有版本称为 DOSASCOMP）：

### 6.7.1 OPSHACOM 记忆法

| 顺序 | 类别 | 缩写 | 例词 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 1 | Opinion/观点 | O | lovely, ugly, beautiful, boring, strange |
| 2 | Size/大小 | S | big, small, tall, tiny, huge |
| 3 | Physical quality/物理特性 | P | rough, smooth, hard, soft, heavy |
| 4 | Shape/形状 | Sh | round, square, flat, triangular |
| 5 | Age/年龄/新旧 | A | old, young, new, ancient, modern |
| 6 | Color/颜色 | C | red, blue, green, dark, pale |
| 7 | Origin/来源/国籍 | O | French, Chinese, American, Asian |
| 8 | Material/材料 | M | wooden, leather, metal, silk, plastic |
| 9 | Purpose/用途（通常是分词） | (P) | sleeping (bag), running (shoes), writing (desk) |

**实际示例：**

```
a lovely little old rectangular green French silver whittling knife
（一把可爱的、小的、旧的、长方形的、绿色的、法国产的、银质的削刀）

实际句子示例：
    a beautiful tall young Chinese woman      （观点-大小-年龄-来源）
    a big round wooden table                  （大小-形状-材料）
    three small black plastic buttons         （数量-大小-颜色-材料）
    an ugly old brown leather bag             （观点-年龄-颜色-材料）
    a lovely little black cat                 （观点-大小-颜色）
```

> **学习建议：** 不需要死记 OPSHACOM 全部顺序，只需记住：观点形容词靠近说话者（在最前），材料/用途形容词靠近名词（在最后）。其余顺序在大量阅读中会自然内化。

## 6.8 中英形容词使用差异对比

### 6.8.1 形容词的谓语用法差异

```
汉语：形容词直接作谓语，不需要系动词
    这本书很有趣。（有趣 = 谓语）
    她非常聪明。

英语：形容词作表语必须通过系动词
    This book is very interesting.    ✓
    ❌ This book very interesting.
    She is very intelligent.          ✓
    ❌ She very intelligent.
```

### 6.8.2 形容词重叠与程度副词

```
汉语常用叠词表强调：大大、高高、慢慢
英语用程度副词：
    very big, extremely tall, quite slowly

汉语"很"≠ 英语 very（直译陷阱）：
    ❌ She is very beautiful.（本身没错，但过度使用显得单调）
    ✓  She is strikingly beautiful. / She is absolutely gorgeous.
       She is remarkably beautiful. / She is breathtakingly beautiful.

常用程度副词梯度：
    极度：  absolutely, utterly, completely, perfectly, terribly
    非常：  very, extremely, highly, greatly, deeply, enormously
    相当：  quite, fairly, rather, pretty, reasonably
    有些：  somewhat, slightly, a little, a bit, marginally
    几乎不：hardly, scarcely, barely
```

### 6.8.3 形容词与汉语"的"字结构

```
汉语的"的"字结构对应英语多种情况：

1. 形容词直接前置（无对应词）：
   美丽的花园 → a beautiful garden（不是 a beautiful's garden）

2. 分词作定语（-ing/-ed）：
   令人感动的电影 → a moving/touching movie
   让人兴奋的消息 → exciting news

3. 介词短语后置定语：
   桌子上的书 → the book on the table（不是 the on-the-table book）

4. 定语从句：
   你昨天买的那本书 → the book that you bought yesterday
```

## 练习

### 练习一：代词格的选择

选择括号中正确的代词：

1. Between you and (I / me), this plan won't work.
2. (Who / Whom) did you invite to the party?
3. The winner was (she / her), not (I / me).
4. My sister and (I / me) went to the museum.
5. This is the teacher (who / whom) inspired me most.
6. Everyone should do (his or her / their) best. (现代英语)
7. The company announced that (it / they) would expand. (美式英语)

### 练习二：形容词排序

将括号中的形容词按正确顺序排列：

1. She wore a ___ dress. (red / beautiful / silk / long)
2. He drove an ___ car. (old / Italian / black / sports)
3. They bought a ___ table. (dining / wooden / round / small)
4. It was a ___ building. (stone / old / impressive / gray)

### 练习三：比较级与最高级

用正确形式填空：

1. Today is ___ (hot) day of the year so far.
2. She speaks English ___ (fluent) than her brother.
3. The ___ you practice, the ___ you get. (much / good)
4. This task is ___ (difficult) than I expected.
5. He is by far ___ (talented) student in the class.

### 练习四：改正代词指代错误

改正下列含有歧义或错误的句子：

1. When Mary visited her mother, she was very happy.
2. The teacher told the student that he needed to work harder.
3. I put the letter in the envelope and then mailed it yesterday.

**参考答案：**

练习一：1. me  2. Whom（正式）/ Who（口语）  3. she, me  4. I  5. who  6. their  7. it

练习二：
1. a beautiful long red silk dress
2. an old black Italian sports car
3. a small round wooden dining table
4. an impressive old gray stone building

练习三：1. the hottest  2. more fluently  3. The more, the better  4. more difficult  5. the most talented

练习四（参考改写）：
1. When Mary visited her mother, Mary was very happy. / Mary was very happy when she visited her mother.
2. The teacher told the student, "You need to work harder." / The teacher said that the student needed to work harder.
3. After putting the letter in the envelope, I mailed it yesterday. / I put the letter in the envelope. I mailed the envelope yesterday.

## 本章小结

| 知识点 | 核心规律 | 常见错误 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 人称代词格 | 主格作主语，宾格作宾语，注意 between you and me | between you and I（错） |
| 所有格区分 | 形容词性（my/your）后跟名词；名词性（mine/yours）独立使用 | it's vs. its 混淆 |
| 反身代词 | 只有主语发出动作，宾语承受动作是同一人时才用反身代词 | myself 替代 me（错） |
| some-/any- 系列 | 肯定句用 some；否定/疑问/条件用 any | someone → anyone（否定句中） |
| 关系代词 | who=人主格，whom=人宾格，which=物，that=限制性从句通用 | 非限制性从句误用 that |
| 形容词句法 | 定语前置；表语接系动词；只作定语/只作表语的词须记忆 | afraid, asleep 等作定语（错） |
| 比较级/最高级 | 短词加 -er/-est；多音节词用 more/most；不规则须记忆 | more better（错），less/fewer 混淆 |
| 形容词顺序 | OPSHACOM：观点-大小-物理-形状-年龄-颜色-来源-材料-用途 | 颜色放在来源前等 |

> **学习建议：** 代词指代清晰是英语写作中的高频失分项。写作时，每次使用代词都问自己：读者能 100% 确定这个代词指代谁吗？形容词顺序则建议通过大量阅读来内化，不必强行背诵顺序规则。
