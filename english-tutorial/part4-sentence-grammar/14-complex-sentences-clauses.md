# 第14章：复合句与从句

> "Grammar is the logic of speech, even as logic is the grammar of reason."
> —— Richard Chenevix Trench

如果说简单句是积木的基本块，并列句是将两块积木水平并排，那么**复合句（Complex Sentence）**就是将积木垂直嵌套——一个分句在语法上从属于另一个分句，充当其中的某个成分。从句（clause）是英语语法中表达复杂思想、精确逻辑关系的核心工具。本章系统梳理名词性从句、定语从句和状语从句三大类型，并提供分析长难句的实用方法。

---

## 14.1 从句的本质：一个句子充当另一个句子的成分

**从句（Subordinate Clause / Dependent Clause）**的定义：含有主语和谓语，但不能独立成句，必须依附于主句（main clause）才能表达完整意义的分句。

### 从句与短语的区别

```
短语（Phrase）：无主谓结构
  the tall man（名词短语）
  running fast（分词短语）
  to succeed（不定式短语）

从句（Clause）：有完整主谓结构
  the man who is tall（关系从句，含 who is）
  because she runs fast（状语从句，含 she runs）
  that I might succeed（名词从句，含 I might）
```

### 从句的三大分类

| 从句类型 | 功能 | 引导词举例 | 在主句中的位置 |
|---------|------|-----------|-------------|
| 名词性从句（Noun Clause） | 充当名词的功能（主、宾、表、同位） | that, what, whether, who, how | 主语、宾语、表语、同位语 |
| 定语从句（Relative Clause） | 修饰名词或代词 | who, whom, whose, which, that, where, when, why | 紧跟被修饰词之后 |
| 状语从句（Adverbial Clause） | 修饰动词、形容词或全句 | because, although, if, when, so that... | 通常在主句前或后 |

## 14.2 名词性从句（Noun Clauses）

名词性从句在句子中充当名词所能充当的任何成分：主语、宾语、表语、同位语。

### 14.2.1 主语从句（Subject Clauses）

主语从句充当句子的主语，谓语动词通常用单数。

```
That he survived the accident is a miracle.
What you said yesterday surprised everyone.
Whether she will attend is still uncertain.
How the thief got in remains a mystery.
Who broke the window is unknown.
```

**避免"头重脚轻"：用 it 作形式主语，主语从句后置**

这是正式书面语中极为常见的处理方式：

```
That honesty is the best policy is well known.（可以，但稍显笨重）
→ It is well known that honesty is the best policy.（更自然）

What he did shocked us.（口语常见）
→ It shocked us what he did.（较少见，但可以）
```

### 14.2.2 宾语从句（Object Clauses）

宾语从句是名词性从句中最常见的类型，充当动词或介词的宾语。

**动词+宾语从句：**

```
I believe (that) he is honest.
She knows what happened.
He asked whether/if she would come.
They discussed how to solve the problem.
I wonder who sent this letter.

注意：that 引导的宾语从句中，that 常可省略（口语尤其如此）：
I think (that) you're right.
She said (that) she was busy.
```

**形容词 + 宾语从句（介词前省略）：**

```
I'm sure (that) he'll pass.
She's afraid (that) she might fail.
He's certain that they will agree.
I'm glad (that) you came.
```

**介词 + 名词性从句（只能用 what/whether/who 等，不能用 that）：**

```
I'm interested in what you said.（不能用 that）
She's worried about whether she passed.
He's concerned about how the project will proceed.
```

### 14.2.3 表语从句（Predicative Clauses）

表语从句跟在系动词（be, seem, appear, remain, look 等）之后，说明主语的内容或性质。

```
The truth is that nobody knows.
The problem is whether we can afford it.
The question is who should be responsible.
That's exactly what I was afraid of.
The reason is that she was exhausted.
It seems as if/though the world has changed.
```

**注意：reason 后表语从句不用 because，应用 that：**

```
❌ The reason is because he was late.
✓  The reason is that he was late.
✓  The reason why he was late is that he missed the bus.
```

### 14.2.4 同位语从句（Appositive Clauses）

同位语从句紧跟在某些抽象名词之后，解释说明该名词的具体内容，与其所修饰的名词是同位关系（即等同关系）。

**常见引导词：that（最常用）、whether**

**常见先行名词：** fact, idea, news, belief, hope, suggestion, possibility, evidence, conclusion, proposal, decision, question, doubt, rumor, thought, report, statement

```
The fact that he resigned shocked everyone.
The news that the company went bankrupt spread quickly.
There is a possibility that she may be lying.
The idea that the earth is flat is absurd.
We have no doubt that he will succeed.
The question whether to accept the offer is still open.
```

### 14.2.5 that vs what vs whether 的选择

这是名词性从句中最常见的选择难点：

| 引导词 | 词性功能 | 在从句中的成分 | 使用场景 |
|-------|---------|------------|---------|
| **that** | 纯连词，无实际意义 | 不充当任何成分 | 后接完整句子 |
| **what** | 疑问代词/关系代词，意为"所...的事/物" | 充当主语或宾语 | 后接成分不完整的句子 |
| **whether/if** | 连词，意为"是否" | 不充当成分 | 表示不确定性/选择 |

```
精确区分 that vs what：

that 引导的从句是完整句子：
I know that he left.（he left 完整，that 无实义）
The fact that she won surprised us.（she won 完整）

what 引导的从句中，what 充当成分，从句不完整：
I know what he said.（what 充当 said 的宾语，he said ___ 有空位）
What he told me is confidential.（what 充当 told 的直接宾语）
What surprised me was her calmness.（what 充当 surprised 的主语）

whether 引导的从句：
I don't know whether he is coming.（是否来，完整句子）
Whether she accepts is up to her.（是否接受）
The question is whether we should proceed.

if vs whether：
if 只能引导宾语从句（非正式）：I don't know if he's coming.
whether 可引导所有名词性从句，且更正式：
Whether he comes (or not) doesn't matter.（主语从句 → 只能用 whether）
The question is whether to go.（表语从句 → 只能用 whether）
I'm not sure whether/if she'll come.（宾语从句 → 两者均可）
```

## 14.3 定语从句 / 关系从句（Relative Clauses）

定语从句紧跟在先行词（antecedent）之后，修饰该先行词，相当于一个形容词。引导定语从句的词称为**关系词（relative word）**，包括关系代词（relative pronouns）和关系副词（relative adverbs）。

### 14.3.1 关系代词的选择

| 关系代词 | 先行词 | 在从句中的成分 | 示例 |
|---------|-------|------------|------|
| **who** | 人 | 主语 | The man who called you is my uncle. |
| **whom** | 人 | 宾语（正式）| The man whom you met is famous. |
| **whose** | 人或物 | 定语（所有格） | The girl whose bag was stolen cried. |
| **which** | 物/动物/整个分句 | 主语或宾语 | The book which I read was fascinating. |
| **that** | 人或物 | 主语或宾语 | The person/car that I saw was gone. |

**who vs whom 的选择（语法检验法）：**

```
检验方法：将从句单独提出，用 he/him（或 she/her）替换关系代词：
→ 如果用 he/she，则应用 who
→ 如果用 him/her，则应用 whom

The man who called you is my uncle.
→ He called you（用 he → who）✓

The man whom you met is famous.
→ You met him（用 him → whom）✓

口语中 whom 常被 who 替代：
The man who(m) you met is famous.（口语可接受）
但在介词之后必须用 whom：
The man to whom I spoke was helpful.（正式）
The man who I spoke to was helpful.（非正式，介词移到末尾）
```

**that 的使用限制：**

```
必须用 that（不能用 which 或 who）的情况：
1. 先行词被 only, first, last, every, all, no, any, much, little 修饰：
   He's the only person that understands me.
   This is the best film that I've ever seen.
   All (that) glitters is not gold.

2. 先行词既有人又有物：
   We talked about the people and places that we had seen.

3. 先行词是 something/anything/everything/nothing：
   Everything that she told me was false.

不能用 that（只能用 who/which）的情况：
1. 非限制性定语从句（有逗号）：
   My brother, who lives in Paris, is visiting next week.（不能用 that）
   London, which is the capital of England, is very crowded.

2. 介词之后：
   The hotel at which we stayed was excellent.（不能用 at that）
```

### 14.3.2 限制性 vs 非限制性定语从句

这是英语定语从句中最重要的区别，**一个逗号的差异可以完全改变句子的意义**。

**限制性定语从句（Restrictive / Defining Relative Clause）：**
- 无逗号
- 提供**必要信息**，限定先行词的范围
- 去掉从句后，句意不完整或改变

```
Students who study hard will pass.
（限制"学生"的范围：只有努力学习的学生才会通过；不是所有学生）

The book that I borrowed from you is on the desk.
（限定"哪本书"：就是我从你那借的那本）
```

**非限制性定语从句（Non-restrictive / Non-defining Relative Clause）：**
- 有逗号（从句前后都有逗号，或从句在末尾只在前面有逗号）
- 提供**附加信息**，先行词已经确定
- 去掉从句后，句意完整，仅少了额外信息

```
My sister, who lives in New York, is visiting us.
（先行词 my sister 已经确定唯一；从句只是补充说她住在纽约）
（= My sister is visiting us. She lives in New York.）

Shakespeare, who lived in the 16th century, wrote Hamlet.
（莎士比亚已经是确定的人，从句补充时代信息）
```

**意义对比的典型例子：**

```
对比一：
I have two brothers who are doctors.
（我有两个兄弟，他们是医生 → 暗示我可能还有其他兄弟不是医生）

I have two brothers, who are doctors.
（我有两个兄弟，他们都是医生 → 我只有两个兄弟，两人都是医生）

对比二：
The students who failed the exam were disappointed.
（只有那些不及格的学生很失望 → 限制性，意味着有些学生及格了）

The students, who failed the exam, were disappointed.
（那些学生（全体）失望了，因为他们都不及格 → 非限制性，暗示全班都不及格）
```

### 14.3.3 关系副词（Relative Adverbs）

| 关系副词 | 先行词 | 替换结构 | 示例 |
|---------|-------|---------|------|
| **where** | 地点名词 | = at/in/on which | The city where I was born is beautiful. |
| **when** | 时间名词 | = at/in/on which | I remember the day when we first met. |
| **why** | reason | = for which | I don't know the reason why he left. |

```
关系副词 = 介词 + which：
The city where I was born = The city in which I was born
The day when we met = The day on which we met
The reason why he left = The reason for which he left

注意：reason why 有时被认为累赘，可以省略 why：
I don't know the reason (why) he left.
That's the reason (why/that) I came.
```

### 14.3.4 介词 + 关系代词结构

在正式书面语中，介词通常置于关系代词之前：

```
非正式：The company that she works for is huge.
正式：  The company for which she works is huge.

非正式：The person who I spoke to was helpful.
正式：  The person to whom I spoke was helpful.

非正式：The issue that we are concerned about is serious.
正式：  The issue with which we are concerned is serious.

非正式：The building that he lives in is old.
正式：  The building in which he lives is old.
       = The building where he lives is old.
```

### 14.3.5 定语从句的省略规则

当关系代词在从句中充当**宾语**时，可以省略（限制性从句中）：

```
可以省略（关系代词作宾语）：
The book (that/which) I read was fascinating.
The man (that/who/whom) you met is famous.
The letter (that/which) she wrote was moving.

不能省略（关系代词作主语）：
The book that changed my life was by Tolstoy.（that 不能省略）
The man who called you is my uncle.（who 不能省略）
```

## 14.4 状语从句（Adverbial Clauses）

状语从句修饰主句的动词、形容词或整个主句，说明时间、地点、原因、条件、让步等各种语义关系。

### 九大类型汇总表

| 类型 | 主要引导词 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| 时间 | when, while, as, after, before, since, until, once, as soon as, by the time | Call me when you arrive. |
| 地点 | where, wherever, everywhere | Sit wherever you like. |
| 原因 | because, since, as, now that, seeing that | Because it rained, we stayed in. |
| 条件 | if, unless, provided (that), as long as, on condition that, supposing | If it rains, we'll cancel. |
| 让步 | although, though, even though, even if, while, whereas, however, whatever | Although tired, she continued. |
| 目的 | so that, in order that, lest, for fear that | She spoke slowly so that he could understand. |
| 结果 | so...that, such...that, so much that | It was so hot that we couldn't sleep. |
| 方式 | as, as if, as though, the way | Do it as I showed you. |
| 比较 | than, as...as, the + 比较级...the + 比较级 | She runs faster than I do. |

### 14.4.1 时间状语从句详解

```
when（当...时，表示时间节点或同时发生）：
When the bell rings, class ends.
When she was young, she lived in Paris.

while（当...时，表示持续性动作，常用进行时）：
While I was cooking, he was reading.
While (= Although) I respect your opinion, I disagree.（让步）

as（随着/当...时，表示同步进行的两个动作）：
As I entered, everyone looked up.
As time passed, she grew wiser.

after vs before（先后顺序）：
After she finished the report, she went home.
Before he could speak, she interrupted him.

since（自从...以来，主句常用完成时）：
I haven't seen him since he left.
It's been three years since we last met.

until / till（直到...为止）：
Wait here until I come back.
She didn't stop working until midnight.（not...until = "直到...才"）
Not until midnight did she stop working.（倒装，强调）

once（一旦...）：
Once you start, you can't stop.
Once the damage is done, it's hard to undo.

as soon as（一...就...）：
As soon as she arrived, the meeting started.
I'll call you as soon as I know the result.
```

**时态规则：时间状语从句不用将来时**

```
❌ I will tell you when I will arrive.
✓  I will tell you when I arrive.（时间从句用一般现在时）

❌ After she will finish, she'll call.
✓  After she finishes, she'll call.

❌ By the time he will come, I'll be gone.
✓  By the time he comes, I'll be gone.
```

### 14.4.2 原因状语从句详解

```
because（最强的因果连接，表直接原因，只回答 why 问题）：
She was late because she missed the bus.

since / as（较弱的因果，表已知原因，引出背景信息）：
Since you're here, let's begin.（既然你已经来了...）
As the meeting was cancelled, we had the afternoon free.

注意语用差异：
because 回答"为什么"；since/as 表示"鉴于、既然"（假设听者已知该原因）

now that（既然...，强调情况已经改变）：
Now that you've grown up, you should take responsibility.

owing to / due to / because of + 名词短语（非从句，注意区别）：
She was absent owing to illness.（因病缺席）
Due to the rain, the game was postponed.
```

### 14.4.3 让步状语从句详解

```
although / though / even though（尽管）：
Although/Though it was cold, she went out.
Even though he's rich, he's unhappy.（even though 语气更强，更出乎意料）

while / whereas（表对比，"而"）：
While I enjoy classical music, my brother prefers rock.
She is outgoing, whereas her sister is shy.

even if（即使，假设性让步）：
Even if it rains, I'll go.（不管是否下雨，都会去）
对比：even though（实际让步）：
Even though it rained, I went.（尽管下了雨，我还是去了）

whatever / whoever / however / wherever / whenever + 从句：
Whatever you do, don't give up.（无论你做什么）
However hard he tries, he can't seem to succeed.（无论多努力）
Wherever she goes, she makes friends.（无论去哪里）
```

**尽管 vs 但是：让步从句与并列转折的区别**

```
复合句（让步从句）：
Although she was tired, she continued working.
（although 在从句中，主句是 she continued working）

并列句（并列连词 but）：
She was tired, but she continued working.
（两个地位平等的分句）

错误：不能同时使用 although 和 but：
❌ Although she was tired, but she continued working.
✓  Although she was tired, she continued working.
✓  She was tired, but she continued working.
```

### 14.4.4 目的与结果状语从句

```
目的（Purpose）：
so that + 主语 + can/could/will/would/may/might...
She spoke slowly so that everyone could understand.
He set an alarm so that he wouldn't oversleep.
I wrote it down in order that I wouldn't forget.
She whispered lest she (should) wake the baby.（正式，lest后常用should）

结果（Result）：
so + 形容词/副词 + that...
such + 名词短语 + that...
He spoke so quietly that no one could hear him.
It was such a boring film that I fell asleep.
She worked so hard that she became ill.
There was such chaos that nothing got done.

区分 so that（目的）vs so...that（结果）：
She studied hard so that she could pass.（目的：为了能通过）
She studied so hard that she passed.（结果：学得太努力，以至于通过了）
```

### 14.4.5 状语从句的省略（Reduced Adverbial Clauses）

**当状语从句的主语与主句主语一致时，可以省略从句主语，并将动词改为分词形式（现在分词或过去分词）**，这是书面语中的高级表达方式。

```
省略规则：主语相同 → 省略从句主语 + 将动词变为分词

完整形式 → 省略形式：

While he was walking home, he met an old friend.
→ While walking home, he met an old friend.
→ Walking home, he met an old friend.

After she finished her homework, she went to bed.
→ After finishing her homework, she went to bed.

Because she was exhausted, she went to sleep early.
→ Being exhausted, she went to sleep early.
→ Exhausted, she went to sleep early.（being 也可省略）

Once it is translated, the document will be sent out.
→ Once translated, the document will be sent out.（被动语态省略 being）

注意：主语不一致时不能省略（否则产生悬垂分词 dangling participle）：
❌ Walking down the street, the trees were beautiful.
（trees 不能 walk）
✓  Walking down the street, I noticed the beautiful trees.
```

## 14.5 从句的嵌套与长难句分析方法

真实语篇中的句子常常包含多层嵌套的从句，形成所谓的"长难句"。掌握系统的分析方法是攻克长难句的关键。

### 14.5.1 括号法（Bracketing Method）

将各层从句用括号标注，逐层剥离：

```
分析以下句子：
"The evidence that scientists have gathered suggests that the process
by which the climate changes is far more complex than the models
that were developed in the 1990s predicted."

步骤一：找到主干动词（suggests）
主句主语：The evidence
主句谓语：suggests
主句宾语：[that the process...predicted]

步骤二：标注各个从句
The evidence [that scientists have gathered] suggests
[that the process [by which the climate changes] is far more complex
than [the models [that were developed in the 1990s] predicted]].

步骤三：逐层标注从句类型
- that scientists have gathered → 限制性定语从句，修饰 evidence
- that the process...predicted → 宾语从句
- by which the climate changes → 定语从句（介词+关系代词），修饰 process
- that were developed in the 1990s → 定语从句，修饰 models

步骤四：还原主干
The evidence suggests [that the process is more complex than the models predicted].
核心含义：科学家收集的证据表明，气候变化的过程比1990年代的模型所预测的更为复杂。
```

### 14.5.2 找主干法（Skeleton Method）

快速定位主句的主语-谓语-宾语，忽略所有修饰成分：

```
技巧清单：
1. 找到句子最外层的主动词（非从句中的动词）
2. 找到该动词的主语（可能被定语从句或插入语隔开）
3. 找到该动词的宾语或表语（若为及物动词/系动词）
4. 将所有从句（用括号或下划线标出）视为整体成分

示例：
"The report (that the committee had submitted) claimed (that the findings
(which had been gathered over a period of five years) were inconclusive)."

主干：The report claimed [that the findings were inconclusive].
其余均为修饰成分。
```

### 14.5.3 常见的长难句模式

```
模式一：主语是从句（避免歧义，常用形式主语）
It is widely believed that [educated people make better decisions].

模式二：多重定语从句
The scientist [who discovered the compound] [that later became the vaccine]
[which saved millions of lives] received the Nobel Prize.

模式三：状语从句 + 主句 + 定语从句
Although the results [that the team obtained] were promising, further research
[into the mechanisms [by which the drug operates]] is still needed.

模式四：同位语从句嵌套定语从句
The assumption [that all students [who fail the exam] lack intelligence]
is deeply flawed.
```

## 练习

**练习一：从句类型判断**

判断划线部分是哪种类型的从句：
1. **That the economy is recovering** is good news.
2. She didn't know **who had sent the letter**.
3. The building **that was destroyed in the fire** was historic.
4. I'll go **wherever you go**.
5. The rumor **that he had resigned** proved false.
6. He failed the exam, **which surprised nobody**.
7. **Although she studied hard**, she didn't pass.
8. The reason **why she left** is unclear.

**练习二：选择正确的关系词**

用 who/whom/whose/which/that/where/when/why 填空：
1. The woman _____ bag was stolen reported it to the police.
2. Is this the park _____ you used to play as a child?
3. I met someone _____ you recommended.
4. The reason _____ she refused is unknown.
5. He was awarded the prize, _____ made his family proud.
6. This is the hotel _____ we first met.
7. Everything _____ she said turned out to be true.

**练习三：改正错误**

1. The reason is because he was nervous.
2. The man which called you is waiting outside.
3. My father, that works in a bank, is very busy.
4. I don't know whether or not if she's coming.
5. Although she was tired, but she kept working.
6. Being walked down the street, the rain started.

**练习四：分析长难句**

用括号法标注以下句子的从句层次，并找出主干：
"The discovery that a small group of researchers who had been studying the
virus for years made suggests that the treatment which was developed based
on their findings may be far more effective than anything that has previously
been tried."

**练习五：状语从句省略**

将以下句子中的状语从句改为分词结构（注意检查主语是否一致）：
1. While she was reading the report, she noticed several errors.
2. After he had eaten dinner, he went for a walk.
3. Because she was not satisfied with the results, she repeated the experiment.
4. Once the document was signed, it became legally binding.

## 本章小结

| 从句类型 | 核心要点 | 常见错误 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 主语从句 | 可用形式主语 it + 后置；谓语用单数 | 主谓一致错误 |
| 宾语从句 | that 常省略；if 只用于宾语从句 | reason is because |
| 表语从句 | 系动词后；reason 后用 that 不用 because | — |
| 同位语从句 | 解释抽象名词内容；用 that 引导 | 与定语从句混淆 |
| 限制性定语从句 | 无逗号；限定先行词范围；可用 that | 误加逗号 |
| 非限制性定语从句 | 有逗号；补充信息；不用 that | 误用 that |
| who/whom | who 作主语；whom 作宾语（尤其介词后）| 主宾混用 |
| 时间状语从句 | 不用将来时；when/while/as 的区别 | 将来时错误 |
| 让步状语从句 | although 不与 but 连用 | although...but 错误 |
| 分词状语 | 主语必须一致；否则悬垂分词 | 悬垂分词 |
| 长难句分析 | 括号法/找主干法；逐层剥离 | 被修饰语淹没 |
